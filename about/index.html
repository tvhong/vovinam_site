Who are we?

UNSW Vovinam is a club that teaches Vovinam Viet Vo Dao, a type of Vietnamese Martial Arts that aims to help build ourselves into the most proficient martial artists we can be. Vovinam Viet Vo Dao encourages us to find a balance between mind and body, harmony with our elders and peers and ultimately inspires us to bring out the best of ourselves.

Why should you join us?

  • It is a great opportunity to enhance your university experience and life by meeting new friends and becoming a part of the Vovinam family.
  • It is an excellent choice for those who want to keep active in a fun and inexpensive way.
  • You will learn a variety of self-defence techniques to increase your safety and confidence.
  • It will allow you to learn about Vovinam Vo Viet Dao itself.
  • Along with the physical and mental benefits, taking part in Vovinam is just great fun!

Who can be member?

We encourage all students and non-students to join, regardless of previous experience and skill level. The club is open to both females and males and people of all cultures. Basically, anyone with aspiration to better themselves is welcome to join!
