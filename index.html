UNSW Vovinam Club was founded in Semester 1, 2013. The purpose of the club is to make Vovinam Viet Vo Dao accessible to UNSW students and people of the Eastern Suburbs.
In order to stay true to the vision of our martial art founder, Master Nguyen Loc, we endeavor to pass on Vovinam knowledge to help individuals grow in strength, confidence and character to face the challenges of the modern world.
